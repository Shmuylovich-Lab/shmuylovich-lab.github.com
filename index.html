The Shmuylovich Lab
Our mission is to help transform dermatology by developing optical imaging technologies that open a new window into understanding skin disease. We seek to leverage infrared imaging to quantitatively assess disease across the full spectrum of diverse skin types, and at length scales ranging from whole body photography to virtual histology.
